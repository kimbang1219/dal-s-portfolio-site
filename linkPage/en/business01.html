Business

Business Structure

It consists of the differentiation of synthetic resins and chemical additives and high-performance/high value
new concept products.

  • 1. High-functional resin(Phenol rein, Acryl reisn, Triazine resin)

  • High-Functional Polymers

    - Resin for electric/electronic material

    - Resin for building material

    - Resin for Automobile

    • 01. Electronic Material Use
    • - Triazine resin : High-functional thermosetting resin: Material for advanced industry such as CCL and semi- conductor package
    • - Cresol, Phenol, Fluorene Novolac Resin for Printing and Semi-conductor material use.
    • 02. Building Material Use
    • - Eco-friendly products which include extremely low emission of TVOCs and Formalin
    • 03. Automobile Use
    • - Acrylic Resin for Filter media, PVAc, Phenolic Resin
    • - Tire Code Adhesive
    • 2.Fine Chemical

  • Fine Chemical

    - High Functional Additives

    - Fine Chemical Intermediate

    • 01. High Functional Additives
    • - Releasing Agent
    • - Curing Agent
    • - Flame Retardant
    • 02. Fine Chemical Intermediate
    • - Pharmaceuticals and fine chemical material intermediates.
    • 3. Nano materials

  • Nano materials

    - Nanoclay for polymer

    - nanocomposite

    - Carbon nanotube

    • 01. Nano Coating
    • - Hybrid type corrosion resistant coating
    • 02. CNT(Carbon Nano Tube)
    • - Conductivity is more excellent than carbon black, carbon fiber. Conductive material with high strength.
    • - Raw material of the product requiring heat resistance, chemical resistance and light weight.
    • - Pre-occupying the new material fields of Carbon nano-tube dispersing agent and master batch manufacturing.
    • 03. Nanoclay
    • - Polymer nanocomposite
    • - Nanoclay that is made from purifying or organificating Montmorillonite
    • - Product of Southern Clay Product of USA(Cloisite)
