· Address : 35, Jungmungwangwang-ro 72beon-gil, Seogwipo-si, Jeju-do South Korea, Lotte Hotel Jeju

How to get there?

By taxi

Catch a taxi at the taxi stand across the airport. Most taxis are medium-sized passenger vehicles

but are charged the same as a general taxi. Taxi fares from the airport to the hotel are fixed,

so confirm the fare prior to departure.

By rental car

In case of car rentals, it is recommended to make a proper arrangement in advance so that the car can be picked up at the airport.

The rental desk and tourist information desk operated by the Jeju Tourism Association are located in the arrival hall.

* Inquiry: Information Desk ☎ +82-64-742-8866
